Features

School Managment App Main Features

The School Managment App comes with ready-to-use features that are completely easy-to-use for any user, even for a beginner.

User Account Management

Priveleges-and-permission based usage. Allows user access to only permitted modules.

Student Information Database

Students enrollments and admissions. Rgeister and view students profile and photo album.

Student Result Management

Record and automatically compute academic results. Set up grading policy to a suit. Dynamically view students results.

Staff Information Database

Manage staff profile records and employments. View staff lists and photo album.

Payments & Billing

Manange all financial payments/billing records. get accurate financial status pf all the students.

Statistics

Dynamically view and monitor statistical analysis of students academic result performance and admission population.

School Calendar System

Organize school academic activities cycle, keep students and staff informed ahead for proper planning and preparations.

Student Attendance

Maintain accurate record of student attendance to school. Monitor and view records in a calendar-like manner.

Assessment/Assignments

Create and publish assessments or assignments for students to access from any part of the world.

Highlights

SMApp Highlights

The SMApp is one of the modern dashboard. It is also a dashboard with high-end features, where users can easily customize or change their projects according to their choice. Please take a quick look at our template highlights.

Switch Easily From Vertical to Horizontal Menu

The SMApp is available in both vertical and horizontal menus. Both menus are managed by single assets. Where users can easily switch from vertical to horizontal menus.

Switch Easily From LTR to RTL Version

The SMApp is available in LRT & RTL versions with single assets. Using those single assets, it’s very easy to switch from one version to another version.

Switch Easily From One Color to Another Color style

The SMApp is available in different types of color styles. Where the users can change their template completely with those color styles.

Switch Easily From Full Width to Boxed Layout

The SMApp is also available in two different types of layouts “Full Width” and “Boxed” Layouts. So that user can switch their dashboard from one layout to another layout effortlessly.

Change Easily Side Menu Styles

The SMApp is also available in different types of Side Menu Styles. Where the users can change their Side Menu styles by using single assets.

Easily From Fixed to Scrollable Layout

The SMApp is also available in two different types of layouts "Fixed Layout" and "Scrollable Layout". Here users can switch their Template from one layout to another layout easily.
